World Population 2022

Demography is an important part of the life of modern society. This science allows you to predict both the decline and growth of the population.

Demographic growth, like decline, often leads to significant problems in a particular habitat. Population growth, problems: food, drinking water, deforestation, poverty of others, etc. Mortality of the population, problems: lack of labor, falling economic indicators of a particular country, etc. Both growth and mortality of the population have both positive and negative sides, so demographic control plays an important role.

This website is designed to control demographic processes both in individual countries and in the world as a whole.
